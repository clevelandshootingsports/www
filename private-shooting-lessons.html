INDOOR SHOOTING RANGE

Our indoor shooting range is air-conditioned, well-lit and built to meet NRA Guidelines. There are 8 shooting lanes, 2 of which are wheelchair accessible. All lanes are at a shooting distance of 25 yards (75 ft.) and equipped with individually retrievable mechanical target systems. An 8 ton safety berm prevents penetration through the target wall.

We offer Class II and III handgun as well as Class III suppressor rental for use at the range. Firearms and accessories are also available for purchase.

We are open weekdays from 9:00am to 7:00pm EST and Saturdays from 9:00am to 4:00pm EST. Stop by anytime.

LANE HOURS, FEES & RULES

RANGE HOURS AND ACCESSIBILITY

Except for holidays and inclement weather, our range is open Monday through Friday from 9:00am to 7:00pm EST and Saturdays from 9:00am to 4:00pm EST. Our shooting range is wheelchair accessible.

DAILY LANE FEES (MEMBERS SHOOT FREE)

Daily fees are $15 for the first shooter and $10 for the second shooter in a single lane. If the second shooter is 13 to 21 years old, he or she may join for just $7.50.

GUN AND SUPPRESSOR RENTALS

If you choose to rent one of our guns, you must also purchase and use our ammunition. Handgun rentals are $15/day and suppressor rentals are $20/day.

RANGE RULES

  • NEVER ASK and NEVER ALLOW anyone to point any weapon at any person for any reason.
  • No more than 2 people per lane.
  • No rifles or shotguns allowed in the range.
  • The Range Safety Officer must approve all guns and ammunition carried in.
  • No one under the age of 10 is allowed at any time.
  • Minors (age 10 to 20 years) must be accompanied by an adult (age 21 years or older).
  • Eye protection, hearing protection and closed-toe shoes are required at all times.

MORE INFORMATION

No experience is necessary for lane use. If you do not know how to use a firearm properly, any of our team members will be happy to show you how to handle your gun safely.

Photos are allowed as long as everyone involved is following safety guidelines and there is no distraction for other shooters, photos are welcome. However, photographers must respect any request from others that they refrain from picture-taking.

Due to necessary ventilation, you may be most comfortable with a light jacket.

MEMBERSHIP PRICING

FOR INDIVIDUALS

Individual Memberships (must be 21+ yrs old) are $40 per month or $250 per year. Membership includes unlimited access to our indoor shooting range and priority lane queu Monday through Friday.

FOR FAMILIES

Family Memberships are $350 per year and the primary member must be 21 years or older. To qualify as a family, you must live at the same residence. Families can be as large as 6 people, all of whom must provide a valid id and proof of age. Membership includes unlimited access to our indoor shooting range and priority lane queu Monday through Friday.
