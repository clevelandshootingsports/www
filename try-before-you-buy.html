TRY BEFORE YOU BUY

Choose a weapon that fits you.

Choosing the right firearm is a very personal decision. Whether you're a first-time gun buyer or looking to add to your existing arsenal, chances are you'll want to try it before you buy it. (We understand that.) At Cleveland Shooting Sports, we offer a unique try-before-you-buy experience. We offer a rental program that allows you to fire a gun or one like it before you make a purchse decision. You can also just rent guns to shoot without any commitment to buy.
