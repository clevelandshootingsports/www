MEET THE EXPERTS

TRAVIS MOREY

Travis is our lead gunsmith and finishing/refinishing specialist. He has been an avid hunter and gun enthusiast all his life. If you want your weapon custom-painted, Travis is the guy - he's a wizard! Travis is trained and certified by DuraCoat, Glock, Core 15 and Double Star.

TYLER SCISSOM

A US Army Ranger's son, Tyler was exposed to firearms at a very young age and taught proper shooting technique and gun safety from the start. Tyler is a regular 3 Gun, IDPA, and IPSC competitor, currently holding three 3 Gun medals. He is one of our TN Carry Permit instructors as well as our resident NFA geek. Want to know about Silencers, SBR's, or Machine Guns? Talk to Tyler!

MATT BRIAN

Matt is our expert machinist. He can make anything to the most rigorous specs! Truth is, he manufactured an item for a NASA Space Shuttle (but we can't tell you what it is - shhhh!). He's our "go-to" guy for creating custom suppressors. Matt is ready to take the most challenging of custom jobs!
