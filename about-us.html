MEET THE EXPERTS

TRAVIS MOREY

Travis is our lead gunsmith and finishing/refinishing specialist. He's been an avid hunter and gun enthusiast for most of his life. If you want your weapon custom-painted, Travis is your guy. He has been trained and certified by DuraCoat, Glock, Core 15 and Double Star. Travis is a master at his craft.

TYLER SCISSOM

Son of a US Army Ranger, Tyler was exposed to firearms at a young age, quickly learning proper shooting techniques and gun safety. He's a regular 3-Gun, IDPA and IPSC competitor, currently holding three 3-Gun medals. He's also an instructor and our resident NFA geek with expertise in silencers, SBR's and machine guns.

MATT BRIAN

Matt is our expert machinist. He can make anything and to the most rigorous of specs! He once manufactured an item for use on a NASA space shuttle, but of course, we can't share the details. Matt's our go-to guy for handcrafting the perfect suppressor and he's always eager to conquer any challenging custom projects!
