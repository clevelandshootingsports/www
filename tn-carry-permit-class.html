TN CARRY PERMIT CLASS

Classes always available

We generally have two classes available every month. Basic handgun knowledge is required as well as 50 rounds of ammunition, a handgun and eye/ear protection. We have all of these items available for rental or purchase, however we encourage you to bring your own gear for the best experience. You should plan to spend the entire day in class.

If you are 21+ yrs old: "Certificate of Completion" is mandated by the State of TN to apply for a Concealed Carry Permit.

Our prep class is open to 16 years and older and each student will receive a "Certificate of Completion" upon completion. Please remember, the State of Tennessee mandates you be 21 years old to obtain your permit.

The class fee is $65 and members receive $15 off. A $20 non-refundable deposit is required to reserve your seat. If you must cancel, your $20 deposit will be credited toward a future class. Also, because our classroom capacity is 25 students, so we must reserve the right to cancel your seat if necessary. In this case, your $20 deposits may be credited toward a future class or used for in-store purchase.

Max. Class Size: 25, Time: 4 hrs. instruction; 4 hrs of additional support, if needed.
